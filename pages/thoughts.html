monolith vs microservices

the app-building question of the day.
but when you put aside the buzzwords, what does it really mean for your app and your development?

here are a few useful questions to ask:

- how will the application be packaged? be able to answer this question for both the monolith and microservices flavors.

- is the desire to use microservices because you want to ship portions of the app independently? if so, how will your build system support this?

- what will the interfaces and communication between services look like? how do you enforce non-breaking apis between services?

- what are the dependency rules for the services? (it's pretty easy to end up with an incomprehensible dependency graph)

- is each service required to maintain an internal api and external api?

- what will the development environment look like? will the development environment require all services to be running, or only a subset?
